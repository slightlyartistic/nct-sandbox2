Who am I?

Hello! I'm Nick Cruz.

Like many, I followed a traditional life-path. I got an MBA in Healthcare Administration, found a job in my field, and worked the 9 to 5 grind. And to be honest, I was content.

Trading started as just a hobby. I wasn't expecting to make loads of money. I only started learning and developing my skills because I found it fun!

I started posting my thoughts and ramblings on Twitter. It was a casual outlet, but I started started stacking followers and the pressure started to build. I start getting messages and tweets from people thanking and crediting me for their success. Eventually, I started getting requests to make a Discord channel.

Which leads us to now! Please explore the website for more info.
